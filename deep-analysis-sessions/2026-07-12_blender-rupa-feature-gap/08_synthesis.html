What the comparison establishes

01

General editable mesh and attributes are the immediate modeling foundation missing beneath Blender-style direct editing, UVs, sculpt, weights, and procedural geometry.

02

A generic non-destructive operation graph is required before adding dozens of modifier-shaped one-off commands.

03

Agent speed does not create missing semantics. A typed capability must exist before UI, CLI, Agent, or MCP can expose it.

04

DCC, media, and simulation systems belong in higher modules; placing them in Swift-CAD would collapse the current responsibility boundary.
